Introduction

Welcome to RemodelWise AI

RemodelWise AI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RemodelWise AI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

Your privacy matters to us. We collect only what is necessary to provide you with the best interior design experience and never sell your personal data to third parties.
Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

Photos & Images: Room photos you upload for AI redesign processing. These are used solely to generate your design and are not stored permanently.
Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features used, designs generated, and time spent in the App.
Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
Purchase Information: Details of in-app purchases and subscription status (payment details are handled by Apple and never stored by us).
Crash Reports: Diagnostic data to help us identify and fix issues with the App.
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

RemodelWise AI uses the collected data for the following purposes:

To provide, maintain, and improve our AI interior design service
To process your room photos and generate AI redesign results
To manage your account and subscription
To send you important notifications about the App and your account
To analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience
To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or other harmful activities
To comply with legal obligations
To respond to your support requests and inquiries
Section 03

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We use trusted third-party companies to help operate the App (cloud hosting, analytics). They are bound by confidentiality agreements.
Google AdMob: We use AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device data to show relevant ads. You can opt out via your device ad settings.
Apple: In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
We never sell your data. Your room photos and personal information are never shared with advertisers or sold to any third party.
Section 04

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Room photos you upload are processed securely and deleted from our servers after your design is generated
All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
We store your data on secure servers with restricted access
We retain your account data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
Section 05

Children's Privacy

RemodelWise AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Section 06

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate data we hold about you
Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data (subject to certain legal exceptions)
Opt-out of Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
Delete Account: You can delete your account at any time from within the App settings
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nasir.developer@icloud.com and we will respond within 30 days.
Section 07

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
